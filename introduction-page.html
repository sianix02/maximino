Introduction

Maximino Noel Memorial National High School is a public high school located in the city of Carcar, in the province of Cebu,Philippines. It offers a comprehensive secondary education program to its students, with a focus on academic excellence, leadership development, and community service. The school is known for its dedicated and highly-qualified

faculty, as well as for its modern facilities and resources that support students in their learning and personal growth. Its graduates are well-prepared to pursue higher education or enter the workforce with the skills and knowledge necessary to succeed.
